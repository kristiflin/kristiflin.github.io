Tempo: Loan Servicing Platform

Tempo is a loan management platform developed by Stream Innovations, the sister company of Power Home Remodeling, dedicated to providing point-of-sale financing for home improvement and solar installations. Over its four-year lifetime, Stream has processed over $2 billion in home improvement loans. As the first and sole UX designer for this greenfield project, I have been instrumental in designing and optimizing user workflows to streamline the approval of millions of dollars in loans daily.
